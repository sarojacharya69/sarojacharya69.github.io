Historical Places of Nepal

Nepal is the land of temples and monasteries. A haven for adventure and adrenaline enthusiasts with an array of amazing places to visit ranging from mountains, forests, valleys, and more.


Here is the list of five Historical Places in Nepal:-

  • Pashupatinath Temple
  • Chitwan National Park
  • Bhaktapur Durbar Square
  • Sagarmatha National Park
  • Lumbini, the birth place of Lord Gautam Buddha
